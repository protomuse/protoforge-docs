## How it works

The full pipeline completes in under 10 seconds.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search the catalogue">
    Find tracks by BPM, key, genre, mood, territory, and availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a license">
    Specify usage type, territory, and duration in a single API call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a contract">
    Deterministic assembly from 146 pre-approved legal clauses — same inputs, same output.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect signatures">

</Steps>
